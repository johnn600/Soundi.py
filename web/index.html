Tempo (Beats Per Minute)

Tempo in music refers to the speed at which a piece of music is played. It measures how fast or slow the pulse of the music is, and it is typically expressed in beats per minute (BPM). Imagine a metronome ticking away, helping musicians stay in time. The tempo determines how quickly or leisurely those metronome ticks occur.
